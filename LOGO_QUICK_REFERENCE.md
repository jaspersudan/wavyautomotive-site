## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Always use SVG format** - Scales infinitely without quality loss
2. **Keep aspect ratio** - Logo is designed with specific proportions
3. **Maintain white space** - Don't crowd the logo with other elements
4. **Use full logo** - Include company name for brand recognition
5. **Icon for small spaces** - Use `logo-icon.svg` when space is limited
